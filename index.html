I am a first-year Ph.D. student in Information and Communication Engineering at the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). My research focuses on preference optimization for LLM-based recommender systems (大模型推荐系统的直接偏好优化), with a particular interest in addressing optimization collapse and improving alignment efficiency. Prior to my Ph.D., I obtained a Master's degree in Big Data Engineering at USTC (2023-2025) and a Bachelor's degree from the Innovation Class of Computer Science at Hefei University of Technology (2019-2023). I also spent a year as an intern at Meituan working on advertising algorithms and LLM-based research.

review		 DynamicPO: Dynamic Preference Optimization for LLM-based Recommender Systems
Xingyu Hu, et al.
WSDM 2026 (Under Submission)
Proposes DynamicPO, which identifies boundary negative samples via real-time clustering and assigns a per-sample β based on the bilateral margin between positive and hard/easy negatives, mitigating optimization collapse in LLM-based recsys. Improves HitRatio@1 by up to 17.6% on three benchmarks.
