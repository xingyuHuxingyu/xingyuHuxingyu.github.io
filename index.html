I am a first-year Ph.D. student jointly trained by the Shanghai Innovation Institute (advised by Prof. Wenqi Shao 邵文琪) and the University of Science and Technology of China (advised by Prof. Xiaojun Chang 常晓军 at the Future Media Lab). My research focuses on agentic AI and preference optimization for large language models, with particular interest in LLM-based recommendation and tool-using agents. My first-author paper DynamicPO received the Best Paper Award at DASFAA 2026 (Top 1 of 834 submissions). Prior to my Ph.D., I obtained a Master's degree at USTC (2023-2025) advised by Prof. Xiangnan He (何向南) at the Lab for Data Science, and a Bachelor's degree from the Innovation Class of Computer Science at Hefei University of Technology (2019-2023). I also spent over a year as a research intern at Meituan Ad Algorithm Team.
